Guns

It is estimated that there are 393 million guns -- or 1.2 guns per capita -- in circulation in the United States, in which most of them are classified as unregistered firearms. To put this ludicrious number into perspective, the country with the second highest guns per capita and a population of at least 30 million is Canada, with 0.347 guns per capita.
